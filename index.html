Our Story

More than just
a coffee shop

Osprey Café is a true neighbourhood spot — a place where you can start your morning with a perfect espresso, linger over a pastry, or settle in for the afternoon with your laptop. We're right at the corner of Queen W and Roncesvalles, and we're here until midnight.

From coffee and baked goods to breakfast sandwiches, natural wine, and even a hair salon — we're a little bit of everything this corner of Parkdale needs.
